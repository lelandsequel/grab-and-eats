The Story

Grab & Eatz is the official food truck of Memorial Trail Ice House — Houston's premier craft bar established in 2019. We bring the same quality and care to our food that Memorial Trail brings to their drinks. Whether you're catching up with friends, watching the game, or just grabbing a bite — we've got you covered.
