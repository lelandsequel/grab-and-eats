THE STORY

Grab & Eatz is the official food truck of Memorial Trail Ice House — Houston's premier craft bar established in 2019.

We bring the same quality and care to our food that Memorial Trail brings to their drinks. Every wing, every burger, every steak is made to order with the ingredients you'd expect at a restaurant — not a typical food truck.

Whether you're catching up with friends, watching the game, or just grabbing a late-night bite — we've got the good stuff.
